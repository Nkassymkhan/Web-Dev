Travel to Lamu Island

Most people traveling to Kenya cannot wait to see the country’s diverse array of wildlife and stunning national parks and reserves However, visitors also should not miss out on the little-known (but incredible) Lamu island, which is located just off the coast in northern Kenya.

Places to visit

  • The streets of Lamu Old Town
  • Kizingo

Besides the sights...

  • Lamu has a sizeable artisan community
  • Carpenters are involved in boat building and making ornate doors and furniture.
  • Lamu’s Arab influences and traditions are audibly apparent by the daily calls to prayer
  • Lamu is home to 23 mosques including the Riyadha Mosque, which was built in 1900
